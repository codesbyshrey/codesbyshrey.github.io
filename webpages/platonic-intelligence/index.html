Fractured vs. Factored Representations

The "Platonic Representation Hypothesis" suggests that models converge toward the same structure when they align with reality. Fractured representations rely on shortcuts and collapse under perturbation. Factored representations align with the world's dynamics, enabling true agency and exaptation.
