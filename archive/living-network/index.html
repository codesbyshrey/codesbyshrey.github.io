The Somatic Web: Fascia

Fascia is not merely a mechanical binder; it is a complex, viscoelastic communication network that permeates the entire body. Research suggests it acts as a "storehouse of consciousness" and a fundamental medium for bodily awareness.

The Ecological Web: Mycelium

Parallel to fascia, mycelial networks act as "Earth's Internet." These vast underground fungal networks facilitate nutrient exchange and signal ecological health.

Structurally, mycelia closely resemble the interconnected nature of fascial systems. They are functional, communicative, and critical for the health of the host organism (the forest), just as fascia is for the human body (Adstrum & Nicholson, 2019).
