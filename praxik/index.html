Healthcare Product

Praxik

A healthcare AI product incubation track focused on communication workflows, clinical decision-support concepts, cloud infrastructure, and product architecture for care-adjacent systems.

Product brief Related NBME study

Focus

  • Healthcare communication tools
  • AI-enabled workflow concepts
  • System architecture and product scoping
  • Cloud infrastructure and rapid prototyping

Product Direction

Praxik sits in the same product family as NBME and clinical roleplay work: tools that support better communication, safer AI behavior, and more legible clinical workflows.

Implementation Lens

The project emphasizes technical feasibility, architecture choices, cloud foundations, and AI product patterns that can move from concept to usable prototype quickly.

PRD Template

A full Praxik PRD would define the target workflow, buyer or user segment, AI safety boundaries, data handling model, integration assumptions, and MVP release criteria.

Related Evidence

The NBME case study and Clinical Roleplay PRD show the more developed version of this product thinking: scenario design, evaluation loops, role fidelity, and feedback architecture.
